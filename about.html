Meet The Team

Brandon aka "Sprout"

Currently a law enforcement officer with 8 years experience in Texas. Brandon is majoring in the Bachelor's of Science Computer Science program at SNHU Online, in his junior year. Brandon's interests are in cybsersecurity as a penetration tester, and forensic computer technician. Brandon wants to develop programs to assist law enforcement officers in their daily tasks. Brandon is our fearless OTR leader who also enjoys woodworking projects, writing programs, and spending time with his two young sons and beautiful fiancee.

Brandon recently started competing in Cybersecurity challenges, and has found a new hobby to add to his free time. In a former life Brandon went to college on a music scholarship (percussion) and studied Music Education and Sound Recording. Brandon still plays drums, bass guitar, rhythm guitar, sings, and even does songwriting from time to time. Brandon is looking forward to graduating SNHU and starting his new career in Cybersecurity/Software Development, hopefully by May 2020.

Matt aka "Matt"

A recent SNHU grad. Matt earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Technology with Cybersecurity concentration. Matt got a job with a company he interned with and is now a Jr. Technical Analyst where he develops and maintains software that supports open source data collections for cyber threat intelligence analysis. (Yeah, we aren't really sure what he does either other than sit behind his computer and drink coffee, but he's the Python Pro! So if you have any Python questions, ask Matt!)

Matt recently returned to SNHU in the graduate program for his Master's degree. Matt enjoys teaching and assisting other students as a mentor and coach for NCL.

Gina aka "Hockeychik"

A Junior at SNHU online and recently switched to the new Cyber Security major. Gina loves to play ice hockey and is a goalie in the local United Women's Hockey League. Gina is a member of the LGBT+ League at SNHU. Gina has two children and is married to her wife, who also plays hockey.

Computers have always been a fun hobby and Gina finally decided to spin her career around and into a field she loves. After participating in her first NCL (National Cyber League) season, she was hooked!

Liam aka "Ghostinthemachine"

Originally an English major but decided to morph into infosec instead. Liam received an Associate of Science in Information Technology from Southern New Hampshire University and is currently working towards a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science also at SNHU and is eyeing up a Masters in Cyber Security (sounds like someone is trying to be a lifelong student!)
